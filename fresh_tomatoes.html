My Movie Trailers Favorites - Marvel

Iron Man (2008)

PG-13|Action, Adventure, Sci-Fi|126 min


Storyline: After being held captive in an Afghan cave, billionaire engineer Tony Stark creates a unique weaponized suit of armor to fight evil.

Director: Jon Favreau

The Incredible Hulk (2008)

PG-13|Action, Adventure, Sci-Fi|112 min


Storyline: Bruce Banner, a scientist on the run from the U.S. Government, must find a cure for the monster he turns into, whenever he loses his temper.

Director: Louis Leterrier

Iron Man 2 (2010)

PG-13|Action, Adventure, Sci-Fi|124 min


Storyline: With the world now aware of his identity as Iron Man, Tony Stark must contend with both his declining health and a vengeful mad man with ties to his father's legacy.

Director: Jon Favreau

Thor (2011)

PG-13|Action, Adventure, Fantasy|115 min


Storyline: The powerful, but arrogant god Thor, is cast out of Asgard to live amongst humans in Midgard (Earth), where he soon becomes one of their finest defenders.

Director: Kenneth Branagh

Captain America: The First Avenger (2011)

PG-13|Action, Adventure, Sci-Fi|124 min


Storyline: Steve Rogers, a rejected military soldier transforms into Captain America after taking a dose of a "Super-Soldier serum". But being Captain America comes at a price as he attempts to take down a war monger and a terrorist organization.

Director: Joe Johnston

The Avengers (2012)

PG-13|Action, Adventure, Sci-Fi|143 min


Storyline: Earth's mightiest heroes must come together and learn to fight as a team if they are going to stop the mischievous Loki and his alien army from enslaving humanity.

Director: Joss Whedon

Iron Man 3 (2013)

PG-13|Action, Adventure, Sci-Fi|130 min


Storyline: When Tony Stark's world is torn apart by a formidable terrorist called the Mandarin, he starts an odyssey of rebuilding and retribution.

Director: Shane Black

Thor: The Dark World (2013)

PG-13|Action, Adventure|112 min


Storyline: When Dr. Jane Foster gets cursed with a powerful entity known as the Aether, Thor is heralded of the cosmic event known as the Convergence and the genocidal Dark Elves.

Director: Alan Taylor

Captain America: The Winter Soldier (2014)

PG-13|Action, Adventure, Sci-Fi|136 min


Storyline: As Steve Rogers struggles to embrace his role in the modern world, he teams up with a fellow Avenger and S.H.I.E.L.D agent, Black Widow, to battle a new threat from history: an assassin known as the Winter Soldier.

Director: Anthony Russo, Joe Russo

Guardians of the Galaxy (2014)

PG-13|Action, Adventure, Comedy|121 min


Storyline: A group of intergalactic criminals are forced to work together to stop a fanatical warrior from taking control of the universe.

Director: James Gunn

Avengers: Age of Ultron (2015)

PG-13|Action, Adventure, Sci-Fi|141 min


Storyline: When Tony Stark and Bruce Banner try to jump-start a dormant peacekeeping program called Ultron, things go horribly wrong and it's up to Earth's mightiest heroes to stop the villainous Ultron from enacting his terrible plan.

Director: Joss Whedon

Ant-Man (2015)

PG-13|Action, Adventure, Comedy|117 min


Storyline: Armed with a super-suit with the astonishing ability to shrink in scale but increase in strength, cat burglar Scott Lang must embrace his inner hero and help his mentor, Dr. Hank Pym, plan and pull off a heist that will save the world.

Director: Peyton Reed

Captain America: Civil War (2016)

PG-13|Action, Adventure, Sci-Fi|147 min


Storyline: Political involvement in the Avengers' activities causes a rift between Captain America and Iron Man.

Director: Anthony Russo, Joe Russo

Doctor Strange (2016)

PG-13|Action, Adventure, Fantasy|115 min


Storyline: While on a journey of physical and spiritual healing, a brilliant neurosurgeon is drawn into the world of the mystic arts.

Director: Scott Derrickson

Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 2 (2017)

PG-13|Action, Adventure, Comedy|136 min


Storyline: The Guardians must fight to keep their newfound family together as they unravel the mystery of Peter Quill's true parentage.

Director: James Gunn

Spider-Man: Homecoming (2017)

PG-13|Action, Adventure, Sci-Fi|133 min


Storyline: Peter Parker balances his life as an ordinary high school student in Queens with his superhero alter-ego Spider-Man, and finds himself on the trail of a new menace prowling the skies of New York City.

Director: Jon Watts

Black Panther (2018)

PG-13|Action, Adventure, Sci-Fi|134 min


Storyline: T'Challa, heir to the hidden but advanced kingdom of Wakanda, must step forward to lead his people into a new future and must confront a challenger from his country's past.

Director: Ryan Coogler

Avengers: Infinity War (2018)

PG-13|Action, Adventure, Drama|149 min


Storyline: The Avengers and their allies must be willing to sacrifice all in an attempt to defeat the powerful Thanos before his blitz of devastation and ruin puts an end to the universe.

Director: Anthony Russo, Joe Russo

Ant-Man and the Wasp (2018)

PG-13|Action, Adventure, Sci-Fi|118 min


Storyline: As Scott Lang balances being both a Super Hero and a father, Hope van Dyne and Dr. Hank Pym present an urgent new mission that finds the Ant-Man fighting alongside The Wasp to uncover secrets from their past.

Director: Peyton Reed
